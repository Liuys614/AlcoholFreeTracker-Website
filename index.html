Privacy by design

Your personal tracking does not need an audience.

Alcohol Free Tracker works without an account or network connection. The current app does not send your profile, check-ins, notes, or drinking history to the developer.

  • ✓Stored on deviceCore tracker data is saved locally using iOS storage.
  • ✓You control backupsExport happens only when you choose to create a JSON backup.
  • ✓No tracking SDKsThe current release contains no advertising or third-party analytics SDK.
